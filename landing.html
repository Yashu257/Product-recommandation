FAQ

Questions answered

Is there really a free trial with no credit card?
Yes. Every paid plan includes a full 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You only get charged if you decide to continue after the trial ends.
Which social platforms does Cadence support?
Cadence currently supports X (Twitter), LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, Threads, Reddit, and Bluesky. We're constantly adding new platforms based on customer demand.
How does the AI writing feature work?
Our AI Studio is trained on your existing content and brand voice guidelines. It generates platform-native content — optimized captions for Instagram, threads for X, thought leadership for LinkedIn — all in a style that sounds like you, not a robot.
Can I manage multiple brands or clients?
Yes. The Agency plan is built for client management with separate workspaces per client, white-label reporting, and team collaboration features. You can switch between client accounts in one click.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account settings with no questions asked. If you cancel before your billing period ends, you'll retain access until the end of that period.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Cadence is SOC 2 Type II compliant and all data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell your data or use it to train third-party AI models. Your content is yours, always.
